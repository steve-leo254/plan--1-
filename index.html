About US

We are Your Florist Shop, a small family-run local business with deep roots in Cairville. For the past 5 years, we have been serving the community with our passion for floristry and a commitment to exceptional quality
Our journey in the world of flowers spans over 25 years, allowing us to bring a wealth of experience and expertise to every arrangement.

We are not just florists; we are floral artists dedicated to creating the perfect floral expressions for your special moments.


Our quaint and charming florist shop is nestled at 223 Bolsover St, where our doors are always open to welcome you. We take pride in offering an extensive selection of fresh flowers and foliage, each handpicked and carefully curated to ensure the highest quality. Every arrangement we craft is tailor-made to suit any occasion, from birthdays and anniversaries to weddings and everyday joys.As we embrace the digital age, we are excited to bring our floral treasures to the online world. Our goal is to make your experience with us even more convenient and accessible. With just a few clicks, you can explore our beautiful floral creations and place your order for next-day home delivery, as long as it's made by 3 pm the day before.
